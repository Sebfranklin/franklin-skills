## QA Checklist

1. **Hierarchy**: Is the primary Call to Action (CTA) the most prominent element?
2. **Spacing**: Is the "Vertical Rhythm" consistent? Are margins and padding uniform?
3. **Color**: Do colors meet contrast ratios? Does the palette follow the user's lead?
4. **Responsiveness**: Does the layout hold up at 320px, 768px, and 1440px?
5. **Interactive States**: Do buttons have hover, active, and focus states?
